About Me

My interest in development started early in high school when I did a graphic media course and was always working with various software from Adobe. One day I found out you could make games in Adobe Flash (RIP Flash) and decided to give it a shot.
I quickly got hooked and learned about Unity3D and I started to do it as a hobby.

After high school, I decided to continue develop my development skills in College and studied Web and Game development for 3 years,
there I got experience in HTML, JavaScript, PHP, CSS, C# and C++.

Nowadays I started using Unreal Engine 5, making games with the Blueprint function and C++ scripts. I am also learning Blender to adjust and export models for Unreal Engine 5 and strive learn to make my own models one day.
Besides development, I enjoy learning new languages, traveling, Photography and play videogames in my free time.
